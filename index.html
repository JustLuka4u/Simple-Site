About Us

Leon Noel is an internationally recognized software developer and entrepreneur. Before joining the Resilient Coders team, he co-founded SocialSci, a scientific surveying company relied on by over 4000 academic institution and held an associate position at Harvard University. Besides his lecturing work at Harvard Business School, MIT Sloan School of Business, and General Assembly, Leon has also worked with major brands such as Shawmut Construction, BMW and Capital One to help train their engineering teams and bring new technology to market. His technological pursuits have been featured in numerous publications including Tech Crunch, Black Enterprise, and the Boston Globe.
